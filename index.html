Dr. B.R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology, Jalandhar

Dr. B.R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology, NIT Jalandhar has been placed in the band of 11th- 25th Rank by ARIIA Rankings 2020. Also, the Institution Innovation Council (IIC) has been established at the institute by Innovation Cell, Ministry of Education, Govt. of India to promote innovation and startups on campus. NIT Jalandhar was formerly known as Regional Engineering College and is well known for its B.Tech program, specially the Computer Science and Engineering specialization. The NIT Jalandhar admission in B.Tech is offered on the basis of rank secured by the candidates in JEE Main followed by JoSAA Counselling process. Students of CSE and ECE branch of B.Tech get the maximum number of placement offers by Microsoft and Amazon, respectively. Around 77 % of students got placed last year in the NIT Jalandhar placement with an average package of INR 8.57 LPA.
